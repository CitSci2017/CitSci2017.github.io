After you are done with the notebook, use these links to gather data to make new graphs.

PyGal documentation: Explore the other kind of graph that you can make.

Speed Test: Take this speed test, write your score on the board and make a plot that includes data from the rest of the class. Be ready to argument why you think this is the appropriate graph for this data.

Hand-Eye coordination Test: Take this test a total of 5 consecutive times and keep track of your scores. Write them on the board. Make the graph that would best describe your scores. Then make a graph that would allow you to compare across individuals.
